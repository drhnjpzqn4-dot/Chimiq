Image display rules:
In the dropdown: show a small circular product photo (40×40px, object-fit: cover, border-radius: 50%, subtle border 1px solid #E0EDE0) to the left of each product name. If the image fails to load (onError), show a neutral placeholder — a soft green circle with a small leaf or flask icon (Lucide FlaskConical), same size.
In the scanner card, when a product is selected: show the product image larger (96×96px, border-radius: 12px, object-fit: cover) to the left of the product name heading. This replaces any generic icon. The product name sits to the right of the image: bold, 18px, #1A1A1A.
In the results section: keep the same 96×96px product image anchored to the top of the results card, next to "Results for: [Product Name]".
For custom scans (user uploads own photo or pastes ingredients without selecting from dropdown): after the user takes/uploads a photo of the product front, display that photo in the same 96×96px slot. If only ingredients are pasted with no photo, show the flask placeholder.
Image loading: lazy-load all product images (loading="lazy"). Do not block the UI or scan function on image load. Images are purely presentational.
