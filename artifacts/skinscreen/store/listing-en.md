## Description (4000 chars max)

**Decode any skincare label in seconds.**

SkinScreen reads ingredient lists, finds combinations that fight or harm your skin, and tells you exactly what to do instead. Built by chemists, refined by a community of 25,000+ skincare nerds, and free to use every day.

**What it does**

• **Single scan** — Paste, snap, or scan a barcode. We highlight every flagged ingredient with the source it came from (FDA, EWG, peer-reviewed papers).
• **Compare two products** — Find out if your serum cancels out your moisturiser before you buy it.
• **Routine cross-check** — Add up to 10 products to your shelf and we audit your full routine.
• **Verified product browser** — Search 12,000+ moisturisers, serums, cleansers, sunscreens with our "Verified Safe" badge.
• **Top mistakes & worries** — Bite-size guides on retinol layering, fragrance sensitivity, photosensitisers, and 23 more topics.

**Built for sensitive, mature, young, and pregnant skin**

Tell us your skin type once and every analysis adapts. We flag pregnancy-incompatible actives (retinoids, salicylic acid >2%) automatically.

**Crowdsourced. Open. Free.**

Every ingredient list you contribute earns you progress toward free Premium — 30 accepted contributions = 1 free Premium month. The product database is open and verifiable.

**Premium (optional, 49 SEK / month or 490 SEK / year)**

• Unlimited shelf
• AI skincare chat with sources
• Full routine cross-check beyond 2 products
• Personalised "what to use instead" suggestions

Subscribe at app.skinscreen.chimiq.com. Manage or cancel any time at the same address.

**Privacy first**

We never sell your data. Ingredient analyses are anonymised. You can delete your account in-app at any moment.
